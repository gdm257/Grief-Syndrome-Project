A mod that makes that makes the playable magical girls overpowered. It only uses the required files for the mod to work, and Megane Homura only has her base stats adjusted, but not her attack power. For every other magical girl, their attack power has been increased drastically for all of their attacks.

Characters who have the ability to stomp on their enemies can now reliably defeat enemiees by just using their bodies as a springboard with a stomp. The stomping action in question can be performed by pressing the jump button while also holding the downwards directional movement key.

In addition, both versions of Homura have had their timestop lengthened, with Megane Homura having less of a boost than Homura does.

The primary purpose of this mod is to make it easier to get to certain locations for testing purposes without having to go through too much of a struggle, but also keep the characters themselves familiar enough to be used comfortably, and on short notice if needed.
