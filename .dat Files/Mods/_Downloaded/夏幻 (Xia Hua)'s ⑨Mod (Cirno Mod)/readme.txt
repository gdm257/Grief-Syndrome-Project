The Cirno mod made by Xia Hua. This mod works by allowing the player to select Cirno by pressing up on the character select screen on any character except for Homura. In addition, only one Cirno can be active at a time. If Cirno already replaces one character, then Cirno cannot replace another character. Since Cirno is classified as a seperate character, if she ends up getting a true death, the character she replaces will not become unusable. However, it will no longer be possible to select Cirno for the rest of that lap.

Cirno's light attack is throwing a small projectle. It deals lows damage, and does not pierce. But it covers the length of the screen. This is Cirno's only light attack.

Cirno's neutral raises a black spike from below in front of Cirno.

Cirno's left and right heavy fires a short burst infront of Cirno. It deals moderately low damage, but has a sweet spot for high knockback, as well as a sour spot for minimum reverse knockback, and can rapid fired for maximum effect.

Cirno's down heavy brings out a hammer. It has low reach, but can act independently of Cirno.

Cirno's up heavy fires several ice spikes upwards at a diagonal angel. Each deals moderately low damage, but have very high knockback and can be rapid fired.

Cirno's special attack sends out six ice spikes. They deal moderately high damage and high knockback.
