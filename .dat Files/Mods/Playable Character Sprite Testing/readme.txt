A simple mod that alters some sprites.

Sayaka has green hair in Gertrud's barrier.

Kyouko has turqoise hair in Gertrud's barrier.

Homura has silver hair in Gertrud's barrier. 

Madoka has inverted colors in Gertrud's barrier. It also gives her glowing eyes, heels, and parts of her uniform as well in Elsa Maria's barrier.

Megane Homura has manually altered sprites to test out if the palette are mandatory for character sprites to be edited or not. From what has been seen, palettes are not required for them to work, but they do add an extra layer to atmospheric depth.
